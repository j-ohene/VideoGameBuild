Mario

Mario made his first appearence in the original Donkey Kong in 1981. His character has went on to become the main protagonist and namesake in many more Nintendo games.

Mario is one of many characters in the game players can select to in each go-cart race. In Mario kart players can choose which arena series they would like to race in whether thst be in single player or multiplayer.

Luigi

The younger and taller brother of Mario who first appeared in Mario Bros in 1983. His chatacter is considered to be the sidekick of his brother and is distinguished by his green clothing and cowardly demeanor.

Luigi is one of many characters in the game players can select to in each go-cart race.

Baby Peach

Formally known as Princess Peach,she made her first appearence in the franchise in 1985 in Super Mario Bros. This cuter version is a minor character in the franchise who made an appearence in Mario & Luigi: Partners in Time as the two main characters time traveled.

Given the popularity of the game the younger version of her character became one of the many avatars players can select.

Baby Daisy

Formally known as princess of Sarasaland, Daisy made her first appearence in Super Mario Land 1989. She is considered a recurring character however her infant eqivalent, is a minor character who debuted in Mario Kart:Double Dash.

Her infant counterpart, Baby Daisy was created specifically to be an playble avatar in Mario Kart

Toadette

Toadette's character was created under similar circumstances to Baby Daisy; orignally Toads racing partner in Mario Kart: Double Dash. She eventually went on to be a playable supporting character in many more games in the franchise and even becomes a main in Captain Toad: Treasure Tracker.

Toadette remains one of the core playable characters in Mario Kart
